Introduction

The Art of Asking ChatGPT for High-Quality Answers: A Complete Guide to Prompt Engineering Techniques
– Ibrahim John

  • ChatGPT is currently the most advanced language model capable of generating human-like text.
  • This book is a comprehensive guide designed to help you understand and utilize various prompting techniques in order to get high quality answers from ChatGPT.
  • The book uses easy-to-understand language to explain and is supplemented by examples and formulas for questioning techniques.
  • Learn how to use prompting techniques to control the output of ChatGPT and make it produce text the way you want.
  • Examples are provided of how to use a combination of different prompting techniques to achieve a specific purpose.
